Find Answers to Common Questions

What is CORT?
CORT provides furniture rental services for homes, offices, and events, offering flexibility and convenience.
How does furniture rental work?
You can rent furniture for a specific duration, with options to opt-in, opt-out, and return whenever needed.
Can I customize my rental package?
Yes, CORT allows you to customize your furniture rental package according to your needs and preferences.
What are the payment options?
We accept credit/debit cards, online payments, and other flexible payment methods.
What is the minimum rental period?
The minimum rental period is typically one month, but it may vary depending on your selected furniture package.
Do you offer delivery services?
Yes, we offer delivery and setup services to ensure your furniture is ready to use.
What happens if I damage the rented furniture?
You are responsible for any damage to rented furniture. Additional charges may apply for repairs or replacements.
