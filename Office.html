        From one-, three-, or six-month leases for space experimentation to accommodating
        seasonal interns to ramping up a response center during a natural disaster, there are

Private Office

We maximize the flexibility of private offices so multiple leadership styles can thrive. These highly customizable furniture collections form the foundation of your operations with desks, seating, credenzas, and shelving that perform at the executive level.

Open Work Area

Think outside the cube with innovations and customizations for open workspaces. While flexibility still comes from a modular approach, personalization with power sources, sit-to-stand desks, and mobile privacy panels opens up new possibilities for the way each individual employee wants to work.

Conference Room

High traffic. High impact. Conference rooms serve many purposes, from gathering consensus and generating ideas to garnering new clients. Create an environment that serves all these business needs and drives progress with mobile and stationary tables, seating, and accents that promote comfort and collaboration.
